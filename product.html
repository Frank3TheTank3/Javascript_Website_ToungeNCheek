London

Meet the Queen

Organize a day trip to Buckingham Palace and take a picture with the royal guards or have a walk in the royal park

Shopping in the city

The metropolitan area is full of brands and big companies offering you everything you can think of. There are also good restaurants all over town which your host can recommend.

Dance in one of the famous clubs

The nightlife in London is exceptional. There are parties every night and if you have a certificate you can enjoy a fun time with drinks and many other party-goers.

The British Musuem

Full of treasure's from around the world, this museum is escpically interesting for tourist with backgrounds in archeology or foreign cultures and their history.

Double decker busses

London is famous for these iconic red busses and we offer to organise a Hop-on hop-off bus tour. This is another great way to experience the London Eye, the Big Ben or St Paul's Cathedral.

The Shard Skyscraper

At 244m height, the Shard rises high above the London skyline and is the perfect opportunity to spot attractions from afar and gives you a unique perspective on the city.

London is the capital city of the United Kingdom. It is the U.K.’s largest metropolis and its economic, transportation, and cultural centre. London is also among the oldest of the world’s great cities, with its history spanning nearly two millennia.

Watch a football game

Britain is known for it's great football teams and culture surrounding this world-class sport. Go meet the fans and take a day to visit a national game.
